The maths

To bank 100,000 profit in a day with 5,000 stakes, each winning slip must return enough profit (not total return).

Daily rules

  1. Only 60%+ picks from Pro Punter — never force a bet when the app has no qualifier.
  2. No slip cap — keep placing 5,000 stakes until profit target or a 5-loss streak ends the session.
  3. 1st half: enter between 15′–20′ on 1H under markets when fusion says BET / STRONG BET.
  4. 2nd half: enter between 60′–65′ on 2H under markets with the same filter.
  5. Skip red-card games, virtual/esoccer, and student leagues (auto-excluded in app).
  6. Session reset: only after 100K profit or 5 losses in a row — then start a fresh session.
  7. Split the target: aim for 2–4 winning accas, not one miracle longshot.

  • Building wealth sustainably usually means smaller daily % on bankroll, not fixed huge targets.
